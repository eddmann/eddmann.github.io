Projects

Outside of work, I enjoy developing small projects to learn new technologies, showcasing them here. Some of my favorites from recent years include:

  • 3x3x3 Rubik’s Cube Solver: A project written in Rust to algorithmically solve a standard Rubik’s Cube.
  • Our Wedding Website: A RSVP web-application - built using DDD, CQRS, Hexagonal Architecture, Event Sourcing, and deployed on AWS Lambda.

Podcasting

From 2013 to 2019, I co-hosted the Three Devs and a Maybe podcast, where we delved into various aspects of software development. Initially focused on web technologies, the podcast evolved to cover a broad spectrum of topics, allowing me to engage with and learn from some truly remarkable individuals.
