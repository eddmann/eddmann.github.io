Installing Nginx/Apache, MySQL, PHP 5.5 (LAMP) stack on CentOS 6.4

Nov 29, 2013

With the widespread appeal and flexibility of an VPS and Vagrant, a shift from mere FTP access to setting up a fresh installation from scratch has taken effect. Tools like Puppet and Chef are great for certain use-cases (i.e. large deployments, dev-ops teams), but starting with the terminal is your best friend. In this post I will take you through the process of setting up a trivial LAMP stack on CentOS 6.4, with the option to use either Apache or Nginx. Both will take advantage of the features PHP-FPM provides you, via FastCGI.
